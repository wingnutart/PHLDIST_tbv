Rip Tide

Created by MEREDITH KOMOROWSKY

A spicy twist on the perennial favorite - The Bay, Basil and Jalapeno combine for a delicious savory cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz The Bay Vodka
  • 3 oz tomato water
  • 3 wedges Lemon
  • 3 leaves basil
  • 1 slice Jalapeno

Preparation:

  1. Muddle jalapeño, lemon and basil in the bottom of a shaker and fill with ice.
  2. Add THE BAY and tomato water and shake to chill.
  3. Garnish with celery stalk.
  4. * To make tomato water: puree tomatoes and strain through a cheesecloth.

Coastal Breeze

Created by AIDAN DEMAREST

Freshen up your cocktail with a spicy alternative. Keep these handy on a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz The Bay Vodka
  • 3 oz tomato juice
  • 1 oz freshLime Juice
  • 3 dashes cayenne pepper
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • sprig Rosemary
  • Jalapeno
  • 2 olives

Preparation:

  1. Mix tomato juice, lime juice, The Bay, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce in a shaker.
  2. Fill with ice and shake to chill.
  3. Strain into ice-filled Collins glass and garnish with Rosemary, olives, and jalapeno wedge.

Anchors Away

Created by MEREDITH KOMOROWSKY

Put it all together and shake - fast, cold and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz The Bay Vodka
  • 1 oz Honey Syrup
  • 2 oz fresh Red Grapefruit Juice

Preparation:

  1. Muddle cilantro into a shaker and fill with ice.
  2. Add THE BAY, honey syrup and grapefruit juice and shake to chill.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  4. Garnish with a cilantro leaf.

Oyster Shooters

Created by GINA CHESERVANI

If you are a raw oyster lover, wait until you try this Oyster Shooter. You will definitely be in "Oyster Heaven!"

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz The Bay Vodka
  • ¼ oz lemon juice
  • ½ oz fresh Mignonette
  • Freshly shucked Chesapeake Bay Oyster

Preparation:

  1. Combine liquid ingredients in drink shaker
  2. Shake over ice.
  3. Strain into shot glass with freshly shucked Chesapeake Bay Oyster.
